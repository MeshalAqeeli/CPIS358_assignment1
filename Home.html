How to use

What is the Warranty Wallet?

  • A web-based application that securely stores and tracks purchase receipts and warranty information.
  • You can create an account and access to your personal dashboard.
  • Add items by entering:
    • Product name
    • Store
    • Purchase date
    • Warranty duration
  • Upload or attach a photo of the invoice.
  • Automatically calculate warranty status (Valid, Expiring Soon, Expired).
  • Filter items by category (electronics, appliances, etc.).
  • Receive notifications before warranty expiry.
  • The main issue is the difficulty in keeping track of warranty information and expiration dates for purchased items. Existing methods such as storing paper receipts or taking photos on a phone are disorganized and unreliable. When warranty claims are needed, users often realize they have lost the required documents or forgotten the warranty period entirely.

How to use

1Click on the login button to access your account or sign up if you are a new user.
2Once logged in, you can upload your receipts by taking a photo or selecting an image from your device.
3Organize your receipts into categories for easy retrieval later.
